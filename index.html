CrystalCollectors!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four dices below.By clicking on Dice you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to random number ,you will lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each Dice is hidden from you untill you click on it.

Each time when the game starts,the game will change the values of each Dice.
